Data Scientist - Mid to Senior Level

Company: DataTech Analytics
Location: New York, NY / Remote

Position Overview:

• Develop and deploy machine learning models for predictive analytics
• Create data visualizations and dashboards for stakeholders
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define analytical requirements
• Design and conduct A/B tests and statistical experiments
• Build automated data pipelines and ETL processes
• Present findings and recommendations to executive leadership
• Mentor junior data analysts and contribute to best practices
